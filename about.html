Who am I?

I'm a very well-rounded person who views life from various perspectives. I have an extremely strategic approach to making decisions, which means that I calculate every step and look at all possible outcomes. Having a sense of humor is very important to me, as it is a wonderful coping mechanism to stress. When in doubt, smile big and stand tall to make yourself feel better.

What Can I Do?


I have a strong interest in application development, especially on the web side of things. This has led me to hone my skills in front-end web development. Making things easy for the user is my aim when I'm designing and developing an application. If the user can fulfill his/her tasks with the application in the most effective manner plausible, then I know that I have done my job. Facilitating user interaction with an application is a very important component to improving the user experience. In short, I specialize in front-end development, but I can develop software applications in general.

When Did I Take Interest in Computing?


I started taking a strong interest in computing when I first attended RIT. My starting major was actually Civil Engineering Technology, so the switch was a huge change for me. What brought about this drastic change was when I explored different web sites on my first laptop and wondered how they were created. Therefore, I talked to my academic advisor, and now here I am!

Why Me?
